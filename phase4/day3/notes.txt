Day 3 — Relationships
What are Relationships?
In a real database, data is connected:

A User has many Posts (one-to-many)
A Post has many Tags and a Tag belongs to many Posts (many-to-many)
A User has one Profile (one-to-one)

Without relationships you'd write raw JOINs. SQLAlchemy handles this with relationship() — you just access user.posts and it figures out the SQL.
