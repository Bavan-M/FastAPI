What are we building?
A production-ready Auth Service that a real Gen AI company would ship:

Users register/login with bcrypt + JWT
Google OAuth support
Refresh tokens with proper rotation
Token blacklisting on logout
Brute force protection on login
3 roles — admin, user, guest with proper RBAC
API key management — create, list, revoke with tiers
Security headers on every response
Secrets from .env — nothing hardcoded
Consistent error responses throughout
