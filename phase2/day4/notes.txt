Why does structure matter?
Right now all your routes are in one file. In a real Gen AI API you'll have:

Auth routes (/login, /register, /refresh)
User routes (/users/me, /users/{id})
Document routes (/documents/upload, /documents/{id})
LLM routes (/generate, /stream, /embeddings)
Admin routes (/admin/stats, /admin/users)

Putting all of this in one file becomes unmaintainable fast. APIRouter lets you split routes across files and mount them into your main app cleanly.
